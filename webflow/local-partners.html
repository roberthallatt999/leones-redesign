Here's the scoop

We could present different FAQs here if you have something that you think want to communicate specifically about partnerships....

How Often do your Flavors Change?

Short answer: whenever they sell out. It completely depends on the time of the year, popularity of the flavor, and even day of the week (weekends seeing more frequent flavor rotation). Sometimes an ice cream will sell out in a week, sometimes it will sell out in an afternoon!

Are You Open Year-round?

Indeed, we are! During our summer season, we're scooping every day (barring Tuesdays) from noon until 10. During the winter season, we close at 9pm, but are still open each day (except Tuesdays).

Catering and events?

Our mobile scoop-cycle brings fresh, ice cream directly to your gathering.

Where is the Ice Cream made?

All the ice cream is made in-house! We churn up our small batch (and do all of our baking) in our kitchen, which is located right at the back of our Spearfish Creamery.

How are your Vegan flavors made?

Most of our vegan flavors are made with coconut milk, but we also experiment with other forms of non-dairy milks and plant fats. Wondering about a particular flavor? Ask our staff-- they're here to answer any and all questions about our menu!
